A) Common Game Items

    1 Health Potion
    - Restores a portion of the player's health.

    2 Mana/Energy Potion
    - Restores a portion of the player’s mana or energy for abilities.

    3 Ammunition
    - Provides extra ammo for the player's weapon.

    4 Armor/Shield Pack
    - Increases the player's armor or shields temporarily or permanently.

    5 Key
    - Used to unlock doors, chests, or other locked areas.

    6 Treasure Chest
    - Contains random loot such as weapons, armor, or gold.

    7 Money/Coins/Gold
    - Collectable currency used to purchase items, upgrades, or weapons.

    8 Weapon Upgrade
    - Improves the player's current weapon or gives them a new weapon.

    9 Magical Scroll
    - Grants the player a one-time use of a powerful spell or ability.

    10 Map
    - Provides information about the current area, revealing hidden locations or objectives.

    11 Experience Orb
    - Gives the player experience points to level up.

    12 Food
    - Restores a small amount of health or grants a temporary buff.

    13 Trap Kit
    - Can be placed by the player to deal damage to enemies.

    14 Speed Boots
    - Temporarily or permanently increases the player’s movement speed.

    15 Bombs/Explosives
    - Can be thrown or placed by the player to damage enemies or destroy obstacles.

    16 Grappling Hook
    - Allows the player to reach distant areas by swinging or pulling themselves.

    17 Medkit/First Aid Kit
    - Heals a large portion of health, often stored for later use.

    18 Cloak/Invisibility Potion
    - Grants temporary invisibility or makes the player harder to detect.

    19 Magic Crystal
    - Can be used to enhance weapons, armor, or abilities with magical properties.

    20 Backpack
    - Increases the player's inventory space, allowing them to carry more items.

    21 Lockpick
    - Allows the player to open locked doors or chests without a key.

    22 Antidote
    - Cures poison or other negative status effects.

    23 Batteries
    - Used to recharge gadgets or weapons that run on energy.

    24 Compass
    - Points to the next objective or hidden treasures.

    25 Quest Item
    - A special item required to complete a specific task or mission (e.g., sacred artifact).

B) Rare/Legendary Items

    1 Legendary Weapon
    - A powerful and unique weapon that has special abilities.

    2 Rare Gem
    - Used for crafting high-level upgrades or selling for a large amount of currency.

    3 Relic
    - Grants the player a permanent, unique ability.

    4 Phoenix Feather
    - Revives the player after they are defeated.

    5 Time Manipulation Device
    - Allows the player to slow down or stop time for a brief period.

    6 Sacred Totem
    - Provides the player with protection from enemies or restores health over time.

    7 Holy Water
    - Damages or weakens undead enemies, or removes curses.

    8 Artifact of Power
    - Boosts all of the player’s stats (strength, speed, defense, etc.) temporarily.

C) Consumable Items

    1 Healing Herb
    - A natural plant that restores health.

    2 Energy Bar
    - Provides a small boost to health or stamina.

    3 Explosive Barrel
    - Can be thrown or placed to deal massive area-of-effect damage.

    4 Poison Dagger
    - A one-time-use item that poisons an enemy.

    5 Smoke Bomb
    - Allows the player to escape or confuse enemies.

    6 Shield Potion
    - Temporarily adds extra shields or protection to the player.

    7 Stamina Booster
    - Refills the player's stamina bar, allowing for faster movement or special actions.

D) Crafting Materials

    1 Wood
    - Used for crafting structures or items.

    2 Iron Ore
    - A raw material used to craft weapons, armor, or tools.

    3 Magic Dust
    - A rare ingredient used in crafting or spell casting.

    4 Leather
    - Can be used to craft light armor or accessories.

    5 Runes
    - Used to enhance weapons, spells, or equipment with magical effects.

    6 Herbs/Plants
    - Collected for crafting potions or healing items.
